        How to clean

        Suede shoes are dirty and need to be cleaned.  Generally, you choose to use  suede shoe cleaner to apply around the shoes, and then gently dry brush with a  toothbrush.  After dry brushing, rinse with water and dry, wearing the same as  new shoes.  Especially snow boots and suede shoes can be cleaned at any time.

        White sneakers are dirty and cleaned.  You can apply a small amount of toothpaste around the surface of the shoes.  After applying for a minute, gently scrub with clean water.  Then wrap the surface of the shoes with toilet paper, and then dry  the shoes.  Same as shoes.

        If you want to clean black leather shoes, you can choose black shoe polish.   Around the black shoes, first use a dry cotton cloth to gently wipe off the dust,  then apply shoe polish, and start oiling the shoes, so that the shoes will become black and shiny.

        Slippers, ordinary sandals, when washed with water, in order to make the shoes more clean, you can add a small amount of detergent to the shoes, soak for 2  minutes, scrub with a brush, and dry in a well-lit place.
